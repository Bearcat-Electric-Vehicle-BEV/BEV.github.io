Meet the Team!!!


Started in Fall 2019, Bearcat Electric Vehicle (BEV) is a student lead organization that  will be building the University of Cincinnati's first Electric Vehicle. Students will have the  opportunity to hone their engineering and leadership skills while building an innovative  car over the next year. Any donations will help senior engineers graduate with a BEV  Senior Design Project. BEV will be supported by our Internal Combustion Engine team (BCMS) to absorb all  the previous knowledge/experience. BCMS has been competing in FSAE since 1981,  being one of the first teams to compete in the competition series.
